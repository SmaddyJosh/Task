Our Story

Founded in 2015, Sweet Creations began with a simple mission: to make professional baking skills and tools accessible to everyone. What started as a small local bakery quickly transformed into a premier training center and supply shop.

Our founder, Chef Maria, realized that many passionate home bakers were frustrated by the lack of high-quality tools and structured education available to them. To bridge this gap, she launched Sweet Creations.

Today, we have trained over 5,000 students and shipped our carefully curated supplies nationwide. Whether you are a beginner looking to bake your first cake, or a seasoned pro refining your fondant techniques, we are here to support your sweet journey.
